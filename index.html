How It Works:

Step 1: Submit Your Property

Fill out the form on this page and submit your property. We’ll automatically receive your information and begin our review process. We look at county data, recent sales data, and research comparable properties to determine what we can offer you for your property.

Step 2: We make you a cash offer

We will contact you either by phone or email to present and discuss with you our offer amount. Once we have come to an agreement, we’ll prepare a purchase agreement and send it your way.

Step 3: You get paid

Once we receive a signed agreement we will begin the closing process and arrange a closing date with you. Leave all the work to us to close on this property.

Funds will be sent to you by check or wired to your bank account based on your preference!

There is no risk and no obligation and we'll make it as easy for you as we possibly can.

  • Is there a cost for your services?

About The Upstate Land Company

The Upstate Land Company is owned and managed by me, Stephen Canner.  

As an upstate New York resident, landowner, and land investor, I know a little bit about the unique situation we face here.  New York state regulations can be cumbersome. Real estate sales can be slow. Winters can slow things down even more. When I started this company I wanted to focus on the unique needs of the people who own land here. 

I have always loved land, maps, and the outdoors.  When I was younger I worked in agriculture and construction but my recent career has swung toward technology. I love helping people meet their goals, and I love stretching myself to see what I can accomplish. This business combines those skills and values.

I live in the Adirondack foothills in northern New York with my wife Jo and several pets.  Having never had children of my own, philanthropy is my "why".  My big goal in life is to be able to donate money to worthy causes.  

I look forward to showing you how you can get cash for your land to help you reach your goals.
